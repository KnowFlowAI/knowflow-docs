## Setting up your KnowFlow account

Follow these steps to get started with KnowFlow and create your first AI assistant.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create an account">
    Sign up for a KnowFlow account at [app.knowflow.in](https://app.knowflow.in). 
    You can sign up with your email.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Create a organization">
    After logging in, you'll be prompted to create your first organization. 
    
    - Enter a name for your organization
    - Add a brief description
    - Invite team members (optional)
    
    Your organization will serve as the central hub for all your knowledge bases and AI assistants.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Create a knowledge base">
    Next, create your first knowledge base to store information:
    
    1. Go to the **Knowledge Bases** section in the sidebar
    2. Click **Create Knowledge Base**
    3. Enter a name and description
    4. Click **Create**
  </Step>

  <Step title="Add knowledge sources">
    You can import information from multiple sources:

    <AccordionGroup>
      <Accordion icon="file" title="Upload Documents">
        1. Select your knowledge base
        2. Click **Add Sources** → **Upload Document**
        3. Select and upload your files (PDF, DOCX, TXT, MD formats supported)
        4. The system will automatically process and index your documents
      </Accordion>

      <Accordion icon="globe" title="Add Website URLs">
        1. Select your knowledge base
        2. Click **Add Sources** → **Website URL**
        3. Enter the website URL you want to index
        4. Set crawling options (depth, page limits, etc.)
        5. Click **Add**
      </Accordion>

      <Accordion icon="github" title="Connect GitHub Repository">
        1. Select your knowledge base
        2. Click **Add Sources** → **GitHub**
        3. Enter repository details (owner/repo format)
        4. Configure indexing options
        5. Click **Connect**
      </Accordion>
    </AccordionGroup>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Create an AI assistant">
    Now that your knowledge base is ready, create an AI assistant:

    1. Go to the **Apps** section in the sidebar
    2. Click **Create App**
    3. Choose the app type (Web Chat, Discord Bot, etc.)
    4. Enter name and description
    5. Select the knowledge base(s) to connect
    6. Configure appearance and behavior settings
    7. Click **Create**
  </Step>

  <Step title="Deploy your assistant">
    Deploy your newly created assistant where your users need help:

    <AccordionGroup>
      <Accordion icon="browser" title="Web Chat">
        1. Go to your app in the Apps section
        2. Navigate to the **Embed** tab
        3. Copy the provided JavaScript snippet
        4. Paste it into your website's HTML before the closing `</body>` tag
      </Accordion>

      <Accordion icon="discord" title="Discord Bot">
        1. Go to your app in the Apps section
        2. Navigate to the **Discord** tab
        3. Follow the instructions to connect your Discord server
        4. Set channel permissions and bot behavior
      </Accordion>

      <Accordion icon="slack" title="Slack Integration">
        1. Go to your app in the Apps section
        2. Navigate to the **Slack** tab
        3. Click **Connect to Slack**
        4. Follow the authorization steps
        5. Select channels where the bot should operate
      </Accordion>
    </AccordionGroup>
  </Step>
</Steps>
