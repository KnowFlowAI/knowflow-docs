## What is KnowFlow?

KnowFlow is an AI-powered knowledge base and chat platform that helps teams effectively manage, access, and share their knowledge. Using advanced AI capabilities, KnowFlow enables companies to transform their scattered documentation into intelligent, searchable knowledge sources that can be accessed via chat interfaces.

## Key Features

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Knowledge Management" icon="book-bookmark">
    Consolidate and organize documentation from multiple sources including websites, files, and integrations with tools like GitHub, Slack, and more.
  </Card>

  <Card title="AI-Powered Chat" icon="robot">
    Create intelligent chatbots that can answer questions based on your knowledge bases with accurate, contextual responses.
  </Card>

  <Card title="Multiple Deployments" icon="server">
    Deploy your AI assistants anywhere your users need help - website widgets, documentation sites, Slack, Discord, and more.
  </Card>

  <Card title="Analytics & Insights" icon="chart-mixed">
    Track chat performance, identify knowledge gaps, and continuously improve your knowledge base with comprehensive analytics.
  </Card>

## Getting Started

Ready to harness the power of KnowFlow for your team? Follow these steps to get up and running quickly:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create an Account">
    Sign up for a KnowFlow account at [app.knowflow.in/register](https://app.knowflow.in/register)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create a organization">
    Set up your team organization and invite members
  </Step>
  <Step title="Build Knowledge Bases">
    Import your existing documentation and connect knowledge sources
  </Step>
  <Step title="Deploy AI Assistants">
    Create chatbots and deploy them where your users need assistance
  </Step>
</Steps>

Get started with the [Quickstart Guide](/quickstart) or explore our detailed documentation to learn more about KnowFlow's capabilities.
